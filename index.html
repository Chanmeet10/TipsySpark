About Us

At TipsySpark Corp. we take pride in being a full-service general contracting company dedicated to transforming homes and businesses across Ontario. With expertise in painting, kitchen cabinet restoration, renovations, landscaping, and outdoor improvements, we deliver craftsmanship that blends quality with creativity. Our approach is built on integrity, transparency, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, ensuring every project is completed with care and precision. Whether it’s refreshing interiors, upgrading kitchens, or enhancing curb appeal, we strive to create spaces that are beautiful, functional, and lasting. At TipsySpark Corp., we don’t just improve properties — we build trust, value, and lasting relationships with our clients.
